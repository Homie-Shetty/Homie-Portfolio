About Me

Hello, I'm Harshith Shetty — a 3rd year Computer Science Engineering student at Shree Devi Institute of Technology, Mangalore.

I’m passionate about crafting interactive digital experiences that merge code with creativity. What began as curiosity has grown into a focused drive to build projects that are both functional and visually compelling.

My core interests include:
• Frontend development with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript (framework-free)
• Gamified web experiences like pixel RPGs, terminal UIs, and simulated OS environments
• Creative coding using tools like Phaser.js, GSAP, and Canvas API
• Designing immersive portfolios that go beyond presenting information to telling a story

I believe a portfolio should reflect who you are through interaction and design. Outside of development, I enjoy experimenting with UI/UX concepts and exploring how far the browser can be pushed creatively.

I'm currently open to creative collaborations, internships, and freelance opportunities that challenge me to grow and innovate.
